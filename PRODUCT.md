# Product Strategy: mimimusic

## Users
- **Audiophiles & Visual Enthusiasts**: Users who value high-fidelity sound and immersive visual experiences. They want to see their music come to life.
- **Digital Artisans**: Creators who like to tweak parameters, create presets, and customize their interface.
- **Ambient Listeners**: Users who keep the player running in the background or on a second monitor as a piece of digital art.

## Product Purpose
mimimusic is a high-end, immersive audio-visual platform. It transforms listening into a multi-sensory experience using glassmorphism UI, advanced Canvas/WebGL visualizations, and interactive gestures. It's not just a player; it's a digital stage for music.

## Brand Identity
- **Tone**: Premium, Sophisticated, Cutting-edge, Cinematic.
- **Voice**: Professional yet accessible, focused on precision and technical excellence.
- **Core Values**: Immersive Design, Technical Prowess, User Customization, Artistic Expression.

## Strategic Principles
- **Visual-First**: Design should always prioritize the visual impact. The UI serves the visualization.
- **Glassmorphism**: Maintain a consistent, modern glass-like aesthetic across all components.
- **Data-Driven**: Every visual movement should be authentically driven by real-time audio data.
- **Premium Craft**: Avoid generic patterns. Every interaction should feel intentional and high-quality.

## Anti-References
- **Generic Winamp/Foobar clones**: Avoid cluttered, legacy-style layouts.
- **Flat Material Design**: Too sterile for an immersive music experience.
- **Aggressive SaaS patterns**: No intrusive upsells, modals, or "growth" hacks.

## Register
- **Brand**: The design *is* the product. The visual experience is why users are here.
